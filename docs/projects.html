Nexill Retail POS

Manual paper checkouts cost small stores massive amounts of time and money. I built Nexill POS entirely from scratch in C#/.NET to solve this. It replaces paper workflows with a lightning-fast digital checkout system engineered strictly for rapid cashier operations, resilient transactions, and total owner control.

C# / .NET SQL Role Permissions Tax Logic Receipt Workflows Keyboard-first UX

Problem

Traditional selling methods in small stores slow checkout, increase billing mistakes, and create long queues at peak hours.

Solution

Nexill replaces manual billing with a faster POS workflow, resilient transactions, role-based controls, and clean checkout operations.

Impact

Stores process customers faster, reduce operational errors, and build a stronger path toward inventory visibility and analytics.
